Introduction

Carrot disease detection is crucial for agricultural industries. carrots are the farmers' main source of income in the hilly areas, Farmers currently rely on human disease identification capabilities, which are unreliable and lacking in expertise to identify carrot diseases. Therefore, a fully automated approach for accurate disease identification of carrot vegetable is required. As a solution, we hope to develop a model to identify carrot diseases from carrot root as well as carrot leaves.


Research problem


Carrot is a fast-growing and nutritious vegetable cultivated in hilly areas in Sri Lanka. for its edible roots. For the production of good quality carrots, modern technology is not being used to its fullest to detect carrot vegetable diseases in farms. As a result, the farmers face difficulties now and then in continuous monitoring and detecting defects in carrot crops in Sri Lanka.



Main objective


The primary objective of conducting this research study is to develop a machine learning-based model that can identify the diseases of the carrot vegetable.

Specific Objectives


  • To identify a suitable and accurate machine learning technique for the identification and classification of carrot diseases.
  • To apply digital image analysis techniques to recognize and classify carrot diseases and fresh healthy carrots.
  • To develop a user-friendly mobile application
  • To make an alternative or supplement to replace the traditional manual method.

Novelties of the Research Project

The novelty is in developing a machine learning model specifically designed for detecting diseases in both carrot roots and leaves.


The research propose to use several machine learning model specifically tailored for detecting diseases in carrot roots and leaves and select best one and connect it with the mobile application

Our research introduce a new dataset of digital images of carrot roots and leaves, along with annotated disease labels, for training and evaluating the machine learning model.

Research Methodology

Data Collection
capturing images of carrot vegetables of good quality and diseased carrots (Root nematode, Sclerotinia Rot, Black Root Rot, Cavity Spot.) with high clarity, with good brightness, and contrast.
Data Preprocessing
Image preprocessing takes place on the images at the lower level wherever image information consists of distortion and noise, and enhancement of the image is done for further processing.
Data Annotation
Labeling and adding additional information to a dataset. And get an experimental dataset. The algorithm is learned to spot patterns and generate predictions using labeled data.
Training Dataset
Faster R-CNN, identifying carrot types (healthy vs diseased) and locating the carrots. (b) Mask R-CNN, identifying the types of carrot diseases and accurately obtaining the location and shape of the disease.
Test Dataset
Assemble a test dataset of photos of carrots that have been annotated to reflect the various diseases that impact them.
Validation Dataset
Examine the evaluation's findings to find any mistakes or weaknesses in the model's operation
