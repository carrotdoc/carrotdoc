Try before buy

Introduction

A virtual fitting system is a device that allows people to virtually try on clothes without having to physically wear them. It makes use of powerful computer vision, augmented reality (AR), and virtual reality (VR) technology to create a simulated world in which users may see themselves dressed up or applying cosmetics.


A virtual fitting system's major purpose is to improve the online shopping experience by allowing buyers to imagine how a particular clothing or product will appear on them before making a purchase. It tackles typical issues with traditional internet purchasing, such as not being able to try on garments for size, fit, and style.


Virtual fitting solutions strive to bridge the gap between physical and digital retail experiences by delivering a realistic and interactive portrayal of the items.

Research Area


  • Research Problem

    The emergence of online stores has resulted in lesser footfall in physical retail stores. Thereby there is a need to have technological innovations to enhance the shopping experience at retail stores in order to increase the footfall and drive sales. Research is needed to integrate virtual fitting systems with AR and VR technology to provide users with a more immersive and interactive experience. This research can look at new ways to blend the virtual and physical worlds, allowing consumers to try on clothes in an immersive and interactive experience.

  • Main Objective

    The main objective of a virtual fitting system is to provide a realistic and interactive experience for users to try on clothing virtually.
    The system aims to bridge the gap between the physical and digital retail experiences, enabling customers to make more informed purchasing decisions and enhancing their overall shopping experience.

  • Specific Objectives

    1. They can interact with the virtual Clothes naturally, such as sliding a Clothes to different positions on their arms and rotating a Clothes to see it from different angles.

    2. The users can also see how the Clothes fit them in different virtual environments than the current real background.

    3. The users can virtually try on different clothes on a video screen at online

    4. User can try T-shirts, top, bottom clothes in different sizes.

    5. User can try virtually and order it using add to cart.
